ABOUT BASIC BITES

People nowadays are always on the go, so setting aside time to cook a meal can be rare. Basic Bites' mission is to show cooks that making a meal at home is possible, no matter how busy your schedule is. We achieve this by providing recipes that are cheap, easy to cook, and only require 5 ingredients or less. Regardless of if you are a beginner cook, a college student, or a parent, we believe that these recipes will help you avoid eating take-out and broaden your culinary skills!
