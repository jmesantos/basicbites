Italian Baked Chicken 35 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pkg. Good Seasons dry Italian dressing mix
  • ½ tsp. garlic powder
  • 6 boneless skinless chicken breast

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the Parmesan cheese, dressing mix, and garlic powder.
  3. Roll up the tortilla tucking in the sides as you go.
  4. Dip chicken in the cheese mixture and coat well.
  5. Place in a 9" x 13" baking dish. Bake for 20-25 minutes until chicken is fully cooked.

Chicken and Asparagus Penne 35 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • 1 lb steamed asparagus
  • 12 oz. cooked penne
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 lb cubed, cooked chicken breasts
  • ½ cup shredded parmesan cheese

Yields 6 servings

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients into a 9" x 13" pan and stir to combine.
  2. Top with Cheese and Broil for 5 minutes or until cheese is golden.

Chicken Primavera with Pasta 23 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts (cut into bite-size pieces)
  • 2 cups frozen stir fry vegetable blend
  • 10 oz. Italian cheese
  • 2 cups hot, cooked fettucine

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Cook and stir chicken in large nonstick skillet on medium heat 5 to 6 min. or until done.
  2. Add vegetables; cook 2 to 3 min. or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add cooking creme; cook and stir 3 min.
  4. Stir in pasta.
