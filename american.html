Oven Roasted Potatoes 35 mins. • $

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 3 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tbsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 4 tbsp. Parmesan cheese

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Slice potatoes into very thin slices (1/4 inch). big ziploc bag to coat the potatoes.
  2. Toss potatoes with olive oil, Italian seasoning and salt in a ziplock bag to coat.
  3. Place in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with foil. Bake at 450 for 15 -20 minutes, until lightly brown. Top with Parmesan cheese and bake an additional 5 minutes.

Dad's Chicken Sandwich 10 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken breast
  • 4 slices toasted bread
  • 1 ½ tablespoons honey mustard
  • 1 large tomato

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Thinly slice the breast meat.
  2. Evenly spread the honey mustard on 1 side of each bread slice. Lay 3 to 4 slices of tomato on 2 slices. Top each sandwich with the other dressed bread slice.
  3. Serve immediately or wrap and refrigerate until ready to eat.

Black Bean Burger 15 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. black beans
  • 2 tbsp. ketchup
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • ⅓ cup instant oats

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Grease a cookie sheet or line with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mash black beans with a fork
  3. Stir in oats, condiments, and spices.
  4. Shape into patties. Bake for 7 minutes.
  5. Slap into a bun with extra condiments and eat!
