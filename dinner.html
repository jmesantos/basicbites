Beef and Mushrooms30 mins. • $ $ $

Ingredients

  • 1-2 tbsp. of olive oil
  • 1 (8 oz.) pkg. sliced button mushrooms
  • 1 lb. sirloin beef tips or stir fry beef, cut into 1" pieces
  • 1 (10 oz.) package Portobello Red Wine Imagine Simmer Sauce
  • 1 (12 oz.) bag wide egg noodles
  • Rosemary, for garnish

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Heat oil in saucepan over medium-low heat. Add mushrooms. Cover and cook for a few minutes, until softened.
  2. Add beef to mushrooms. Cook over medium heat, until no longer pink. Drain if needed.
  3. Add in simmer sauce. Heat through and serve over hot cooked noodles. Garnish with rosemary, if desired. Enjoy!

Coconut Curry15 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp. red curry paste
  • 2 small heads broccoli
  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • ½ tbsp. cornstarch dissolved in 2 tbsp. cold water

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Saute broccoli (and onion/garlic if you're using it) in a tablespoon of oil.
  2. After a few minutes, add the coconut milk and let simmer for 5-8 minutes. The broccoli should soften but still be tender-crisp.
  3. Add the curry paste to the pan and whisk it until it combines with the coconut milk. Add the chickpeas.
  4. Bring to a slight boil and add the cornstarch. Boil for about a minute, then reduce heat and let cool slightly. Sauce will thicken as the mixture cools.

Walnut Crusted Chicken30 mins. • $ $ $

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless boneless organic chicken breast
  • 1 cup walnuts, finely chopped
  • 2 organic eggs

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Place eggs into a mixing bowl and whisk.
  3. Place walnuts into a separate bowl.
  4. Dip each chicken breast in the egg mixture then in the walnuts bowl to coat the chicken.
  5. Place chicken in a baking disk and bake for 25 min.
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
