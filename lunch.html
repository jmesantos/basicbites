Cheese Toast 15 mins. • $ $ $

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. mayo
  • 2 tbsp. mustard
  • 8 slices uncrusted bread
  • ⅓ cup grated parmesan

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Combine mayonnaise and mustard, spread on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Cut each slice into four triangles.
  3. Place with plain side down on a lightly greased baking sheet.
  4. Sprinkle with cheese.
  5. Broil 4 in. from the heat for 1-2 minutes or until lightly browned.

Baked French Fries 35 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes
  • 3 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 tbsp. parsley

Yields 6 servings

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Toss potatoes and oil in large bowl. Spread on baking sheet.
  3. Place in the oven for 15 minutes. Cook until the fries become golden brown about another 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Remove and sprinkle with salt, pepper and fresh chopped parsley.

Dad's Chicken Sandwich 10 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken breast
  • 4 slices toasted bread
  • 1 ½ tbsp. honey mustard
  • 1 large tomato

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Thinly slice the breast meat.
  2. Evenly spread the honey mustard on 1 side of each bread slice. Lay 3 to 4 slices of tomato on 2 slices. Top each sandwich with the other dressed bread slice.
  3. Serve immediately or wrap and refrigerate until ready to eat.
