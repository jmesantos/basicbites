Granola Bar 20 mins. • $

Ingredients

  • 3 cups oats
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • cup nuts
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • ½ cup honey

Yields 10 servings

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Grease a 9x13 baking pan.
  2. Mix together honey, peanut butter, oats, chia seeds, and nuts. Stir.
  3. Firmly press mixture onto the greased pan. Bake for 25 minutes or until just golden brown.
  4. Allow to cool on wire rack.
  5. Cut into squares and enjoy!

Blueberry Banana Parfait 15 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • ½ cup granola
  • ½ cup vanilla yogurt
  • ½ cup blueberries
  • 1 sliced banana

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Place ¼ cup granola in the bottom of a glass or bowl.
  2. Follow with ¼ cup yogurt and all of the blueberries (and ½ banana if using a full banana).
  3. Then top with remaining granola, yogurt, and banana.

Cottage Cheese Pancakes 15 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • ½ cup oatmeal
  • ½ cup cottage cheese
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 4 egg whites

Yields 4 servings

Directions

  1. Blend all ingredients in blender.
  2. Spray skillet with cooking spray and cook just like"silver dollar" pancakes, a few small ones at a time.
  3. Top with your favorite pancake topping!
