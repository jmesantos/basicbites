Thai Peanut Noodles 20 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. soba
  • 1 packet Thai peanut sauce
  • ½ oz. sesame seeds
  • ½ bunch green onions, sliced

Yields 8 servings

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the directions on the box (boil for 10 minutes or until al dente). Meanwhile, slice the green onions.
  2. Drain the pasta in a colander and then toss with Thai Peanut Sauce, sesame seeds and green onions while still warm. Eat and enjoy!

Fried Rice 15 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • 4 tsp. sesame oil
  • ¾ cup peas and carrots
  • ½ cup chopped scallions
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tsp. soy sauce

Yields 4 serving

Directions

  1. In a skillet, heat sesame oil over medium. Cook, stirring, 1 minute.
  2. Add peas and carrots, scallions and rice.
  3. Stir in soy sauce.

Thai Lime Chicken 35 mins. • $ $

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup dry-roasted peanuts
  • 1 ½ tbsp. garlic powder
  • 1 ½ tsp. ginger, ground
  • ½ lb. chicken breast
  • 1 lime

Yields 2 servings

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F.
  2. Grind peanuts until fine, but not to paste.
  3. In a small bowl, combine peanuts, garlic powder and ginger.
  4. Place chicken on a cookie sheet and squeeze juice from lime overtop. Roll chicken in peanut mixture until well coated.
  5. Bake chicken in oven for 25 minutes.
